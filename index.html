The collected data is used for:

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data is strictly used for internal business purposes.

We retain data only as long as necessary for business operations and legal compliance.

6. User Responsibility

Users of this application are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of login credentials and ensuring proper use of the system.
